Welcome to the Memory Matching Game!

At the beginning of the game, the cards will be arranged on the screen with their faces down.

You need to match pairs of cards by selecting two cards at a time. You can choose from 3 to 20 pairs of cards in the game.

If the cards match, they will remain displayed. If they don't, they will be displayed for 2 seconds and then flipped back over.

You win the game when you have matched all pairs of cards.
